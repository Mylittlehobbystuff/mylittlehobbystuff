About

Hello!

I am an 18 year old student.

I already started making creative stuff when I was young. I am the third generation of family members who make and sell handmade products.

I started with felt and thread, but since 2016 I started crafting with Fimo clay and I was sold!

When I found out people loved personalized jewellery and gifts I started to sell them. The thing I love most are the custom orders, specific wishes that I can make come true, that is my ultimate goal.

Whether you're a student looking to showcase your work, a professional looking to attract clients, or a graphic artist looking to share your projects, this template is the perfect starting point!
