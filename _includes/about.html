About

Hello!

I am an 18 year old student.

I already started making creative stuff when I was young. I am the third generation of family members who make and sell handmade products.

I started with felt and thread, but since 2016 I started crafting with Fimo clay and I was sold!

When I found out people loved personalized jewellery and gifts I started to sell them. The thing I love most are the custom orders, specific wishes that I can make come true, that is my ultimate goal.

Enjoy looking around, and if you feel just as amazed as I do about my collection, feel free to share the enthusiasm and spread the word! That would mean so much to me!

This website is still under construction because I have a new name, logo etc., but thank you for visiting this website! I am still not a professional, just a hobbyist and I am so proud of this website already!

If you want to order something, you can fill in the contract form. Specify which product you would like, mention any desired changes (or describe your custom order) and add a note if it is a gift :) I will then contact you to make an agreement for an etsy purchase.

Enjoy my website!
